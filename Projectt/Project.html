Project: Silent Faced

"Silent Faced" is an exciting and immersive video game project that I have designed and developed. It offers a unique gaming experience with the following features:

  • Genre: 2D Top-down game.
  • Platform: PC.
  • Gameplay Mechanics: Hide from the monster and solve the mystery to find a way forward. If you encounter the creature, you must survive by either fighting back to fend off the monster or running to hide somewhere so that it can't find you. Then, you can continue solving the mystery.
  • Unique Selling Points: We have implemented the terrifying feeling from Alien Isolation. If you want to experience a similar feeling in a game, this is the one.

"Silent Faced" is currently in development and aims to provide players with an unforgettable gaming experience. Stay tuned for updates and release information!

About

A 2D survival horror game.The game offers a tense survival experience where every challenge presents multiple solutions. It emphasizes immersing players in a terrifying and unpredictable atmosphere, encouraging them to fully engage with the in-game world . When players succeed in overcoming obstacles or defeating an enemy, they experience brief moments of relief. However, the enemy is never truly goneâ€”it retreats, only to hide and return later. Exploration plays a key role, providing players with additional strategies for survival.

What I Do

In this project, I was responsible for team management, storytelling, designing game mechanics, and creating level designs to ensure a cohesive player experience. I designed the monster AI behavior, the level layout, and how the guns function in alignment with the story and character development. I provided detailed design briefs to the art team, enabling them to create characters as I envisioned. Additionally, I designed how each item functions and interacts with the environment, determining where items should drop based on the game's pacing. I also orchestrated when enemies would appear to hunt the player. Furthermore, I crafted the game loop, ensuring smooth and continuous gameplay from start to finish.
